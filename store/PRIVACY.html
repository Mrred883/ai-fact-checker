AI Fact Checker

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

AI Fact Checker is a browser extension that checks text, audio, and files using AI services you connect with your own API keys. This page explains exactly what data the extension handles and where it goes.

The short version

  • The extension has no server of its own. Nothing is sent to the developer.
  • Your API keys and your check history are stored only in your browser and never leave your device, except to call the AI providers you set up.
  • Content you choose to check is sent directly from your browser to the AI providers listed below, and only when you start a check.

What is stored on your device

Saved in your browser only, using local extension storage:

  • Your API keys (an Anthropic Claude key, and an optional Deepgram key).
  • Settings such as model choice, sensitivity, language, and theme.
  • Check history, meaning the claims, verdicts, and uploaded files you have checked, so you can review them.

You can clear history and remove files from inside the extension at any time. Uninstalling the extension removes all of this local data.

What is sent to third parties

The extension sends data only when you start a check, and only to the providers below, using the keys you supply.

Anthropic (Claude API)Required

  • What is sent: the text you select or scan, transcripts of tab audio you choose to check, the contents of files you upload, and comments you paste for sentiment analysis.
  • Why: to check claims, verify them with web search, analyze sentiment, and assess AI origin.
  • Endpoint: api.anthropic.com. Governed by Anthropic's privacy policy.

Deepgram (speech to text)Optional

  • What is sent: audio captured from the browser tab you choose to listen to.
  • Why: to transcribe spoken audio so it can be checked.
  • Endpoint: api.deepgram.com. Governed by Deepgram's privacy policy.

The extension does not send your content to any other party, and the developer never receives it.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • storage: save your keys, settings, and history locally.
  • activeTab and scripting: read page or selected text into the current tab only, and only when you click to scan, enable highlight to check, or use the right-click action. The extension has no standing access to any website.
  • tabCapture and offscreen: capture the audio of a tab you choose, for live checking.
  • contextMenus: add the right-click "Fact-check selection" action.
  • sidePanel: show the interface in a docked side panel.
  • Access to api.anthropic.com: call the Claude API. This is the only host the extension can reach.

Data retention, children, and changes

Retention: the developer keeps nothing. Local data stays on your device until you clear it or uninstall. Data sent to Anthropic and Deepgram is handled under their own policies.

Children: the extension is not directed to children under 13.

Changes: this policy may be updated, and the date at the top reflects the latest version.
